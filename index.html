About Me

Journey to the Infinite Learning

From the classrooms of a Bengali medium school in Rampurhat to the world of web development, my journey has been fueled by one thing: passion.

I wasn't born into tech. I grew up in Tarapith, Belia, where my father taught in a primary school and my uncle inspired me as a university professor. I was always the student who grabbed second position, consistently scoring 90% — not because I had to, but because I loved learning.

When I discovered coding, something clicked. The struggle to buy my first PC became my motivation. The desire to do something unique became my fuel. Now, as an English Honors student in my 2nd year of college, I merge my passion for language with the art of code.

"Push Yourself Because No One is Gonna Do that for YOU!" — This is what I live by. I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and DSA not in fancy bootcamps, but through countless hours of learning, struggling, and creating. Every website I build carries this spirit.

What makes me different? I don't just write code — I understand your vision. My background in English helps me communicate clearly, my struggle taught me patience, and my passion ensures every project gets the quality it deserves.
